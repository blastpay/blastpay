How do I use my One Card MasterCard?

You can use your OneCard MasterCard anywhere Debit MasterCard cards are accepted. Be sure to know your card balance before you shop. When you're ready to make your purchase, give the cashier your card or, if asked, swipe your card at the terminal. The purchase amount will automatically be deducted from the card balance.

My available card balance will not cover my purchase amount. Can I still use my OneCard?

Yes, you can still use your OneCard MasterCard. Just tell the cashier in advance how much to deduct from your card and how much you will pay using an alternate payment method.

Note that this process, known as "split tender," may not be supported by all merchants.

What happens if I need to return an item I purchased using my OneCard MasterCard?

When making a return, you may be asked to show the cashier the card used to make the purchase. The value of the returned merchandise will be credited back to your card.
