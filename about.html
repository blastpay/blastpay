BlastPay originates from the union of Citizens Bank of Weir, member FDIC and Yantra Financial Technologies in 2012. Together, they have created innovative payment processing products to not only modernize the payments industry, but also solve all financial inconveniences to improve customers' everyday lives.
BlastPay is a traditional bank account with extraordinary features. Along with ACH and Wire, BlastPay uses cutting-edge technology to enable companies to send money instantly to a recipient's existing debit card 24/7/365. In addition, companies can finally streamline payments to India within just a few hours. This product is the ultimate tool for any company to manage their finances and add various benefits.
